Wednesday, June 29

Copenhagen walking tour (17:30 - 19:00)

The tour will start at Kongens Nytorv and end at Christiansborg Palace. Kongens Nytorv is reachable by metro and is only two stops away from the Islands Brygge station. After getting off at the Kongens Nytorv station, you should locate the guide and the crowd at the square plaza of Kongens Nytorv, on the round plaza with the large statue in the center. For your convenience, there will be a convoy starting from the venue at 17:10 and heading toward the Islands Brygge station.

Banquet at the Tårnet restaurant (19:15 - 21:30)

The Tårnet restaurant is located inside the tower of Christiansborg Palace, where the walking tour ends. With its 106 metres, the Christiansborg Palace tower is the highest in Copenhagen and offers a magnificent view of the city's rooftops. It is free to access the tower. There is a lift from the entrance to the top of the tower. The observation tower is just above the restaurant, which means that you can take the lift up to the top and overlook Copenhagen before or during your dinner.
